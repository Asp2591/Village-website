About the Foundation

Maza Gav Maza Abhiman is a community-driven foundation dedicated to improving our village. The foundation focuses on youth development, sports activities, and village improvement projects. With the support of villagers and donors, we organize initiatives that encourage unity, health, and progress in our community.
